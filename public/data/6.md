OnSite is an all-in-one mobile app to help tradespeople manage their work, site records, and customer history directly from their mobile phone.

The idea originated from my father, who has worked in various trades. A big frustration of his was he had no easy way to recall previous work while on-site or check when equipment services were due. This often wasted time and money for both him and the customer.  

Current solutions include using a combination of cloud storage, photo galleries, and calendars. This fragmented approach increases the likelihood of mistakes and miscommunication, and it doesn’t even include platforms to share these details, such as WhatsApp and generic email. In fact, 70% of tradespeople use this scattered, multi-platform approach.

onSite solves this problem by uniting everything in one place.

# Development

## Beta Testing

The app is currently available on TestFlight, being trialed with tradespeople across County Cork. The official launch date is slated for June 5th 2026. The app will be available for pre-order later in April.

## Programming

It is written in Swift for iOS and iPadOS. The majority of the app is written with SwiftUI, with a few components restricted to UIKit.
