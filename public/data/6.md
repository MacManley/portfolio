OnSite is an all-in-one iOS mobile application purpose-built for tradespeople. It centralises site management, job documentation, scheduling, file management, and client contact information in a single, easy-to-use platform — eliminating the fragmented workflow that costs tradespeople hours every week.

The idea originated from my father, who has worked in various trades. A big frustration of his was having no easy way to recall previous work while on-site or check when equipment services were due. This often wasted time and money for both him and the customer.

OnSite solves this problem by uniting everything in one place.

![OnSite Home Promo](./assets/onsitehomepromo.png)

## The Problem

Around 70% of tradespeople rely on a scattered, multi-platform approach — WhatsApp, SMS, email, photo galleries, files, and calendars — to manage work-related information. This fragmented workflow leads to tradespeople spending over 5 hours per week searching across channels for job details, with 25% of project delays directly caused by miscommunication and lost information.

Desk and field research conducted during the development phase at the SPARK UCC programme estimates tradespeople could lose up to €10,000 per year as a result.

## The OnSite Solution

OnSite replaces that fragmented workflow with a single, purpose-built platform:

- **Site profiles** with full service histories and automatic arrival notifications.
- **Photo documentation** — before, after, progress, and completion shots with captions, auto-sorted by GPS location.
- **GPS-powered contact matching** that links your address book to the right job sites automatically.
- **On-site file management** — attach and preview PDFs, spreadsheets, and documents directly from the field.
- **Smart scheduling** with automated reminders for upcoming services and invoices, skipping weekends, bank holidays, and non-working days based on user input.
- **Overdue notifications**, so you never forget an invoice or service again.
- **Full offline functionality** with automatic sync when connectivity is restored.
- **iCloud integration** with full iPhone and iPad support.

What sets OnSite apart is its GPS-powered intelligence. Arrival notifications alert tradespeople the moment they pull up to a site, reminding them to note site progress, while GPS-powered algorithms match photos and contacts to sites — letting tradespeople set the app up in less than 10 minutes.

## Development

OnSite is written in Swift for iOS and iPadOS. The majority of the app uses SwiftUI, with select components built in UIKit.

The marketing website is built in React, and the documentation site is built with GitHub Actions and Jekyll, both hosted on GitHub Pages.
