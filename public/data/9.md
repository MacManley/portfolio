Reeves was created in just 2 hours alongside [Toby](https://www.linkedin.com/in/toby-tangney-9b732227a/) at UCC X Claude Builder Club's hackathon, and it won €250 in Claude API Credits.

Reeves is a web-based interview preparation platform that takes the stress out of mock interviews. Instead of brutal assessments with no feedback, we built an AI coach that asks tailored questions, analyzes your responses in real-time, and genuinely encourages you when things get tough.

# Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Vite, Vanilla JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
- **API**: Anthropic Claude (streaming)
- **Deployment**: Vercel
- **Repository**: github.com/MacManley/claude-hackathon

# Frontend

The frontend was built with **Vite + Vanilla JavaScript and Tailwind CSS**, ensuring a responsive, lightweight interface that works seamlessly across devices. We prioritized a clean, intuitive design to reduce friction during interview practice. The UI uses large, clear buttons and minimal on-screen clutter—no stressful timers or clinical aesthetics. Navigation is simple and consistent, building user confidence as they progress through the mock interview.

The interview flow is split into distinct stages:

1. **Job Input** — Paste a link to the job posting. Reeves extracts the role and context automatically.
2. **Flow Configuration** — A visual node-based interface lets you arrange questions however you want. Want technical questions first? Done. Behavioral at the end? Easy. This customization removes the one-size-fits-all frustration.
3. **Interview Session** — Questions are delivered one at a time with enough space to think. Streaming responses from Claude mean feedback arrives instantly—no awkward waiting.
4. **Analysis & Feedback** — Each answer gets real-time critique: what worked, what to improve, and encouragement to keep you grounded.

# Backend

**Anthropic Claude API** powers the entire system. The API handles three critical jobs:

1. **Intelligent Question Generation** — Claude receives the full job posting as context and generates role-specific questions across behavioral, technical, and domain categories. This is why the questions feel relevant, not generic.

2. **Real-Time Response Analysis** — As you answer, Claude streams back analysis of your response quality, extracting demonstrated competencies and flagging weak points. The streaming approach keeps latency low—you get feedback in 2–4 seconds.

3. **Encouragement Logic** — If Claude detects uncertainty ("I'm not sure", long pauses, hesitant tone), it triggers supportive prompts. The goal is to make interview prep feel like a coaching session, not an interrogation.
